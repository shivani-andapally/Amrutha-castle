About Us

Amrutha Castle stands as a symbol of royal grandeur. Designed with the opulence of medieval castles in mind, it offers guests an enchanting atmosphere of royalty and class. Its striking architecture, plush interiors, and beautiful landscapes create an unforgettable impression. Every aspect of the hotel exudes luxury, from the moment you step inside until your departure.

Accommodation

Indulge in a stay like no other at Amrutha Castle. From elegant standard rooms to palatial suites, each space ensures comfort and luxury.

  • Deluxe Rooms - Ideal for solo travelers and couples, offering all modern amenities for a pleasant stay.
  • Executive Suites - Spacious rooms with high-end furnishings and breathtaking views.
  • Royal Suites - The pinnacle of opulence with living areas, refined décor, and majestic views.

Dining

Dining at Amrutha Castle is an experience that goes beyond taste.

  • Deluxe Restaurant - A regal dining ambiance with Indian and international delicacies.
  • Castle Café - Casual, stylish café for breakfast, tea, and light meals.
  • Bar & Lounge - Relax with a curated collection of spirits and cocktails in an elegant setting.

Events & Celebrations

Whether it's a fairy-tale wedding or a corporate conference, Amrutha Castle is the ideal venue.

  • Weddings - Lavish or intimate, customized packages to make your big day magical.
  • Meetings & Conferences - Professional spaces with modern tech for productive sessions.
  • Private Parties - Celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, or special events in style.

Wellness & Recreation

Your comfort and relaxation are our priority.

  • Spa & Massage - Rejuvenating treatments combining tradition and modern therapies.
  • Fitness Center - Fully-equipped space for all your workout needs.
  • Swimming Pool - A refreshing spot to relax or enjoy a peaceful swim.

Location

Conveniently located in the heart of the city, Amrutha Castle offers easy access to major attractions, shopping centers, and business hubs. Whether for business or leisure, you're always close to where you need to be.
