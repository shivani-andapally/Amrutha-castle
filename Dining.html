MEET THE CHEF

Meet Our Chef: Chef Arjun Varma With over 15 years of culinary excellence, Chef Arjun Varma brings both passion and precision to every plate. Trained in classical Indian and international cuisines, he has worked in five-star resorts across India and Southeast Asia, mastering flavors that delight every kind of guest from traditional spice lovers to continental foodies. Chef Arjun believes that great food is an experience, not just a meal. His creations are known for their bold flavors, creative presentation, and a deep respect for locally sourced, seasonal ingredients. Whether you're enjoying a serene breakfast by the pool, a candlelight dinner under the stars, or a spicy tandoori platter straight from the grill—Chef Arjun’s dishes promise to turn your stay into a flavorful memory.
